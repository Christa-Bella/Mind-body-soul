Beginner workouts

For beginners, it's important to start with foundational exercise that build strength, improve fitness, and help develop proper form. Here is a list of beginner friendly workout exercises

  • Squats
  • Push-Ups
  • Lunges
  • Plank
  • Glute bridges
  • Walking or jogging
  • Jumping jacks
  • Cat-cow Stretch
  • Child's pose
