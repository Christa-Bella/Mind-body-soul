How to deal with fitness challenges

Fitness is important in our daily, from the physical importance, to the benefits it has on mental healh, and social benefits we still struggle to keep up, in this page I talk about how to deal with challenges of keeping up with your fitness, they include:

  • The first thing is findind ways to stay motivated; you can track your progress, stay inspired by getting a work out buddy, and also remember to celebrate small wins, like finishing your workout, doing it properly
  • Set goals on what to achieve with your fitness routine, do you want to loose weight, gain weight in some areas or just stay, understanding what you are working towards will help you stay motivated
  • Be flexible and adjust your plan as needed based on progress, feedback etc.
These are some of the ways you can overcome quitting your fitness journey.
